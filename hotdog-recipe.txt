Ingredients:
 - 2 sausage
 - 2 pieces of hot dog bread 
 - mayoneise
 - ketchup
 - 1 spoon of butter

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, melt butter and wait until it's warm then put the sausage.
  2. wait until they are cooked.
  3. add some mayoneise to the bread and put the sausage in there.
  4. again, melt butter and put the hot dog to fry the bread a little bit.
  5. add ketchup.
